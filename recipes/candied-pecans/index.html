Candied Pecans

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pecan halves
  • 1 egg white
  • 1 tbsp cold water
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cinnamon

Directions

  1. Add water to egg white and beat until stiff.
  2. Fold pecan halves into the beaten egg white and coat thoroughly.
  3. Mix sugar, salt, and cinnamon well and put in a zip-lock bag.
  4. Add pecans and shake until well coated.
  5. Spread on a cookie sheet and bake at 275°F until lightly browned and dry, 35–40 minutes. Loosen with a fork every 10 minutes as they bake.
