Bran Muffins

Ingredients

  • 1 cup 100% Bran Buds cereal
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 2½ cups flour
  • ½ cup margarine
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1½ cups sugar
  • 2½ tsp baking soda
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups All Bran cereal
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • Raisins and cinnamon, optional

Directions

  1. Mix together Bran Buds and 1 cup boiling water. Set aside.
  2. Cream together the margarine and sugar. Beat the eggs and add to the creamed mixture. Mix in the buttermilk.
  3. Add flour, salt, and baking soda. Mix well. Add the All Bran and the soaked Bran Buds mixture and mix well.
  4. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  5. Fill muffin cups about ¾ full. Add raisins in the middle if you want. Sprinkle cinnamon on top if you like.
  6. Bake 15 to 18 minutes.

Store in a sealed container in the refrigerator for up to 10 days. Great when served with cream cheese (so it isn't too healthy).
