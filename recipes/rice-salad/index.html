Rice Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 boxes Uncle Ben's Long Grain & Wild Rice
  • 1 package Ocean Spray dried cranberries
  • 1 can (12 oz) honey roasted cashews or peanuts
  • 1 red, yellow, or orange pepper, chopped
  • 1 package frozen peas
  • 1 bunch scallions, chopped
  • ¼ cup honey
  • ¼ cup white wine or rice vinegar
  • ¼ cup oil
  • 1 tbsp curry powder

Directions

  1. Cook the rice according to package directions and let cool completely.
  2. Combine the rice, cranberries, cashews, pepper, peas, and scallions. Toss well.
  3. Whisk together the honey, vinegar, oil, and curry powder to make the dressing.
  4. Pour dressing over the rice mixture and stir to combine. Let sit at least 2 hours before serving.

Makes a lot of salad — we typically cut this recipe in half.
