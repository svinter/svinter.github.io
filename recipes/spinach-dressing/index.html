Spinach Salad Dressing

Ingredients

  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tsp mustard
  • ¼ cup lemon juice
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • 1 tbsp tarragon vinegar
  • ⅛ tsp sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Blend all ingredients together and refrigerate.
  2. Serve over a spinach salad with bacon bits, hard boiled eggs, and croutons.

Note: this doesn't taste particularly good on lettuce. Very strange.
