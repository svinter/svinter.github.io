What I do

I work at three levels: individuals, teams, and organizations.

Working with individuals

Coaching is a partnership in being thoughtful and intentional about how we change.

I typically provide a combination of coaching and advising focused on a set of development areas you have identified. At the start, we create a coaching agreement articulating our intentions, responsibilities, and the logistics of working together. We then identify your goals and desired outcomes, decide which assessments to use, and determine where to seek other information about you that would be helpful. We meet regularly, working in parallel on topics you raise and longer-term goals.

Most people begin with an initial focus on either professional goals ("I want to drive more successful outcomes in the organization I'm leading") or personal ones ("I want to be happier and less stressed").

Over time, all roads usually lead to an interconnected set of factors relating to your values, your ambitions, your confidence, your skills, your relationships, and your decision-making.

Being our happiest, best, and most energized and successful selves involves understanding and navigating these different dimensions of ourselves.

I coach CEOs and leaders at a variety of levels in organizations, though I also coach several individual contributors.

Working with organizations

I tend to work with many organizations over long periods. I work with companies in many sectors, including tech, energy, and biotech; startups and large companies; public, private, and nonprofits. Most organizational work grows from coaching and advising work I do with a leader and their team.

Organizational work includes leadership development training, improved teaming, strategy, culture development, organizational design, and change management.

Each engagement is unique: they come in different shapes and sizes, and they evolve.
